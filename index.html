About Me.

Instructional Designer & Web Developer

Hi, I'm Dee Mullins . 👋

In 2017, a CEO took a chance on my skills and moved me into a curriculum developer role. I was working with a custom built LMS, creating courses using Markdown, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. That same year I completed an 8 week program with Girls in Tech using WordPress and PHP and built my first portfolio.

At the beginning of 2018 I was selected to participate in a 12 week Front-End Web Development Google Scholarship challenge partnered with Udacity. I completed in the top 10% out of the 15,000 participants accepted which granted me access to a 6 month Nanodegree in Web Development from Google.

In 2019 I started at Arizona State University working with faculty to create online courses. Based on my background, I handle a lot of the tech aspects which include 3rd party tools, LTI integrations, and creating cool things students can interact with while building on their knowledge and skills. In 2020 I started my M.Ed in Learning Design and Technology program at ASU.

Other than that I love my espresso in the morning, traveling and attending music festivals. To me, it’s all about seeing the world and making memories while doing what keeps a smile on my face. I also want to get stuff done, work hard, and continue building my skills.
